FAQ

Common questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every new account receives a 14-day full Elite trial automatically, no credit card required. After the trial you choose your plan.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Mobile Money (MTN, Moov, Orange, Wave) through KKiaPay — available across Africa and internationally.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no long-term commitments. Cancel at any time and your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are fully GDPR-compliant and you can export or delete your data at any time.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible on the Free plan. If you delete your account, all data is permanently removed within 30 days.
