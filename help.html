Help Center

How can we help you?

Find answers about how Florencia OS works, how to get started, and how to get the most out of the platform.

🚀

Getting Started

Create your free account, set your first monthly revenue goal, and add your first lead to your pipeline. You can be up and running in under five minutes.

🎯

Setting Your Revenue Goal

Your revenue goal is the engine of Florencia OS. Once set, the platform organizes your daily actions, pipeline, and AI suggestions around reaching that number.

👥

Managing Your Pipeline

Add leads to your pipeline and move them through stages — Prospects, Qualified, and Active. Florencia OS tracks the value of each lead and alerts you when follow-ups are due.

🤖

Using the AI Copilot

The AI Strategy Copilot gives you personalized guidance based on your goals and pipeline. Ask it anything about your business — it gives specific, actionable advice, not generic tips.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Florencia OS?+
Florencia OS is a business operating system built for freelancers, creators, and solo entrepreneurs. It combines lead pipeline management, daily action planning, revenue goal tracking, and AI strategic guidance into one focused tool — so you always know what to work on and how close you are to your income goals.
Who is Florencia OS for?+
Florencia OS is built for anyone who works independently — freelancers, consultants, coaches, creators, designers, developers, copywriters, and any skilled professional who generates income from their own work. If you run your own practice and want more structure and predictability, this platform is designed for you.
Is Florencia OS free?+
Yes — access to the beta version of Florencia OS is free. You can create an account and start using the platform at no cost. Pricing for future premium features will be announced before the official launch.
How does the daily action plan work?+
Every day, Florencia OS analyzes your revenue goal, your current pipeline, your pending tasks, and where you are in the month. Based on this, it generates a prioritized list of actions for the day — so you start each morning knowing exactly what to do without having to figure it out yourself.
Is my data secure?+
Yes. Florencia OS is built on Supabase, a secure cloud infrastructure provider. All data is stored in encrypted databases. Your email address and account information are never sold or shared with third parties. You can review our full Privacy Policy for details.
Can I use Florencia OS on my phone?+
Yes. Florencia OS is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. You can access your pipeline, check your daily plan, and interact with the AI Copilot from any device.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?+
Go to the login page and enter your email address. Then click « Mot de passe oublié ? » — we will send you a password reset email. Check your inbox (and spam folder) and follow the link to set a new password.
How do I delete my account?+
To request account deletion, contact our support team at contactflorenciaos [at] gmail.com with the subject « Account Deletion Request ». We will process your request within 5 business days.

Troubleshooting

📧
I didn’t receive a confirmation email after signing up Check your spam or junk folder. If you don’t see the email after a few minutes, try signing up again with the same address — Supabase will resend the confirmation. If the issue persists, contact our support team.
🔒
I can’t log in even though I have an account Double-check that you’re using the correct email. Try resetting your password using the « Mot de passe oublié ? » link on the login page. If your account was created during the waitlist phase, you may need to create a new full account.
📱
The page doesn’t display correctly on my phone Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Florencia OS is designed for modern mobile browsers. If you’re using an older browser, try switching to Chrome or Safari.
⚡
The AI Copilot is not responding Check your internet connection. If the connection is stable and the issue persists, try logging out and back in. If the problem continues, please contact our support team so we can investigate.
